Privacy Policy for TextGenieAI

Last Updated: June 1, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to TextGenieAI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use our TextGenieAI application for macOS.

Information We Don't Collect

TextGenieAI is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data, including:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, etc.)
  • The content of your text selections or writing
  • Your usage patterns or behavior within the app
  • Your device information
  • Your location data

How TextGenieAI Works

TextGenieAI operates entirely on your local device. When you select text and use our features:

  • All processing happens locally on your device
  • No data is sent to our servers or any third-party servers
  • No user accounts or registration is required
  • No analytics or tracking mechanisms are implemented

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will provide notice through the application or by other means.
