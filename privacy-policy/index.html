Privacy Policy for Where is my cash ?

Effective Date: December 1, 2023

Welcome to Where is my cash ?. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use our Where is my cash ? application for iOS.

Information We Collect

Where is my cash ? is designed with your privacy in mind. We only collect the minimum information necessary to provide our expense tracking services:

  • Receipt images you scan (stored securely on your device and in iCloud)
  • Expense data you manually enter
  • App usage analytics (anonymized and aggregated)
  • Crash reports to improve app stability

How Where is my cash ? Works

Where is my cash ? prioritizes your privacy while providing intelligent expense tracking:

  • Your financial data is encrypted with bank-level security
  • Receipt processing uses on-device AI when possible
  • Data syncs securely through your personal iCloud account
  • We never sell or share your personal financial information

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will provide notice through the application or by other means.
